Our Promise

Cessa is built on one belief: everyone deserves to be heard. These guidelines protect that space for all.

💛

Be Kind

  • Treat every person with respect and empathy
  • Remember: the person on the other end is real, with real feelings
  • If you disagree, do so gently
🌱

Be Present

  • Give your full attention during sessions
  • Listen as much as you speak
  • The questions are guides, not scripts — follow where the conversation naturally flows
🛡️

Be Safe

  • Never share personal information (real name, phone, address, social media)
  • Never ask for personal information from your partner
  • If someone makes you uncomfortable, use the Panic Button immediately
🚫

Do NOT

  • Use hateful, discriminatory, racist, sexist, or homophobic language
  • Harass, bully, stalk, or threaten anyone
  • Share sexual, violent, or graphic content
  • Attempt to meet users outside of Cessa
  • Record, screenshot, or share conversations
  • Use the platform while intoxicated
  • Promote products, services, or ideologies
🆘

If You Need Help

  • In-app Use the Panic Button to end any session instantly.
  • Report After a session, tap "No" on the safety check to flag the conversation.
  • Crisis If you or someone you're talking to is in danger, contact local emergency services.
