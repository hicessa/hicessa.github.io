Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Applies to Cessa mobile application

👋 Overview

Cessa ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and the rights you hold over it.

Our commitment: We collect only what is necessary to connect you with another human being. We do not sell your data. We do not record your voice calls. We do not share your information with third-party advertisers.

📦 Data We Collect

Account Data

  • Email address (required for authentication)
  • Display name — pseudonyms are fully supported
  • Language preference
  • Topic preferences (e.g., "Loneliness", "Career", "Relationships")

Usage Data

  • Session history: partner display name, call duration, timestamp
  • Mood logs: a 1–5 rating you provide after each session
  • Safety check responses (Yes / No after each session)
  • Streak count and engagement metrics

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your real name — your pseudonym is sufficient
  • Photos or profile pictures
  • Precise or approximate location data
  • Voice call content — calls are real-time only and never recorded or stored
  • Contact lists or social media accounts
  • Device microphone access outside of active calls

⚙️ How We Use Your Data

🛡️ Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) servers located in the United States, protected by Row-Level Security policies that ensure you can only access your own data.

  • Authentication uses OAuth 2.0 and JWT (industry-standard protocols)
  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS / TLS 1.2+
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID / Fingerprint) is processed entirely on your device — this data never reaches our servers
  • We conduct periodic security reviews and follow responsible disclosure practices

🗓️ Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Active account data Retained while your account is active
Session history 90 days, then permanently deleted
Mood logs 1 year, then permanently deleted
Safety reports 2 years (safety compliance requirement)
Deleted accounts All data permanently erased within 30 days of deletion request

⚖️ Your Rights

Regardless of where you live, you have the following rights:

  • Access — view all data we hold about you via the Profile screen
  • Portability — request a machine-readable export of your data by contacting us
  • Correction — update incorrect account data at any time in-app
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data from the Profile screen
  • Opt out — disable AI-powered features by contacting support
  • Withdraw consent — you may withdraw consent at any time; this will not affect processing that occurred before withdrawal

GDPR (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area, the legal basis for processing is your consent given at signup. You may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. Our Data Controller is Andy Cristofer, reachable at cessahearth@gmail.com.

Indonesia UU PDP

For users in Indonesia, we comply with Undang-Undang Perlindungan Data Pribadi (UU PDP). You have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

🧒 Children's Privacy

Cessa is intended exclusively for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we discover that an account belongs to a person under 18, that account will be terminated and all associated data deleted immediately.

📢 Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification at least 14 days before they take effect. Continued use of the app after changes are in effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
