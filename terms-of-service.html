💛 Community

Community Guidelines

Your safe space, our shared responsibility.

🔥 Our Promise

Cessa is built on one belief: everyone deserves to be heard. These guidelines protect that space for all of us.

"The fire inside you is not meant to burn alone."

💛 Be Kind

  • Treat every person with respect and genuine empathy
  • Remember: the voice on the other end belongs to a real person with real feelings
  • If you disagree with something, express it gently — you don't have to agree on everything to connect

🎯 Be Present

  • Give your full attention during sessions — put your other apps away
  • Listen as much as you speak
  • The conversation starters are guides, not scripts — follow where the conversation naturally flows

🔒 Be Safe

  • Never share your personal information: real name, phone number, address, or social media
  • Never ask for personal information from your conversation partner
  • If something makes you uncomfortable, use the Panic Button immediately — no explanation needed

🚫 Never

  • Use hateful, discriminatory, racist, sexist, or homophobic language
  • Harass, bully, stalk, or threaten anyone — inside or outside the app
  • Share sexual, violent, or graphic content
  • Attempt to meet or contact users outside of Cessa
  • Record, screenshot, or share any part of a conversation
  • Use the platform for self-promotion, sales, or recruitment

⚡ Consequences

1
First violation Formal warning — you'll be notified and reminded of the guidelines
2

3

🆘 If You Need Help

  • In-app: Use the Panic Button to end any session instantly, no explanation needed
  • Report: After a session, tap "No" on the safety check to flag the conversation for review
  • Crisis: If you or someone you're talking to is in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services

Every time you show up with kindness, you make Cessa safer for someone who really needs it. Thank you for being part of this community.
